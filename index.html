In this blog I'll be analyzing different and mostly random Kaggle datasets with the goal of

For the first few posts I'll probably choose some datasets I'm already acquainted with (at least w.r.t. its general topic). I'll proceed to exploring other, random datasets, where I choose to filter for sports, music or biology related ones (mostly).
